Common questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a referral to book?
No referral is needed. You can book directly — starting with a free 15-minute Meet & Greet if you'd like to connect first.
What ages do you work with?
Adults and elders (65+), seen individually or as couples. Teen counselling is currently offered for ages 16+.
How do child & teen counselling sessions work?
Sessions should be booked under the teen's name. All first sessions are with parent(s)/guardian(s) only.
Do you offer online sessions?
Yes — all sessions are available virtually, serving clients anywhere in Alberta, in addition to the Calgary, AB T2Z location.
Is therapy confidential?
Yes. What's shared in session stays confidential, with the standard legal exceptions around safety (e.g., risk of harm to yourself or others) that apply to all registered psychologists in Alberta.
How do I get started?
Book directly through our online scheduler, or try a free 15-minute Meet & Greet first — no pressure, just a chat.
