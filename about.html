You're probably one of many adults wishing you could overcome self-sabotaging beliefs and put the past behind you. Or, maybe you're a couple wanting to get back on track and get through some challenges together. In this confidential, relaxed space, you can be heard without judgment and start to process with professional guidance to spot you along the way.

I use a unique blend of kindness, humour, and evidence-based methods to help you feel seen and known. For couples, I use the Gottman Method. Culture and spirituality are always welcome in our sessions.

The clients I support often come in feeling discouraged, inadequate, or lonely, and then move towards hope, confidence, and connection. Couples often start with rockier communication, intense or buried conflict, and disconnection, and then move towards closeness, skills, and the ability to pivot together.

I'm here to listen and offer a space where your story can unfold at your pace. Let's figure out what matters most to you and help you show up more fully for yourself and those you care about. Book a first session to start — no pressure, just a chat.
